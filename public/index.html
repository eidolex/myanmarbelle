Myanmar Belle Co.,Ltd was founded in 1995 by U Ye Myint Maung and five siblings together.

U Ye Myint Maung is the managing director and the others fours of his siblings are the shareholders(Board of Directors) of Myanmar Belle Co.,Ltd. U Ye Myint Maung is the central executive committee at the republic of the union of Myanmar Federation Of Chambers Of Commerce and Industry. He is also general secretary in Myanmar Food Processors And Exporters Association.

Company Profile

Myanmar Belle Company Limited" was founded in 1995 as "Belle Myanmar Enterprise" and changes its name to the Limited Company in 2004 to be in line with the adoption of the market-oriented economic policy of the government and has formed the following departments:

AGRICULTURE, MANUFACTURE & EXPORT
  • Konjac
  • Dehydrated Vegetables
  • Frozen Vegetables
IMPORT & SERVICE
  • Kose' Cosmetics’ Distribution
  • Logistics
  • Supermarket

We are now distributing our products to the consumers in many countries and we have created a worldwide reputation for our quality and reliability. During the years of our company's growing period, at the behest of the foreign company, some of our departments entered into a new venture of distributing farming products. In addition, for local consumers, modernizations of KOSÉ Cosmetics Branches were implemented to provide better services to our customers. The sales of our products are increased as customers requirement for better performance and reliability increased. For our customers service, our activities are involved in making it easy for customers to reach the right parties within the company and responded quick and satisfactory service, answers and resolutions of problems. We would like to express our sincere gratitude and appreciation to our entire customers for their continuous support and we intent on developing stronger bonds and loyalty with our ultimate customers.
