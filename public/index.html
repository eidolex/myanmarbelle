AGRICULTURE, MANUFACTURE & EXPORT

We are now distributing our products to the consumers in many countries and we have created a worldwide reputation for our quality and reliability. During the years of our company's growing period, at the behest of the foreign company, some of our departments entered into a new venture of distributing farming products. Company Chairman Introduction

Ye Myint Maung

Chairman

Myanmar Belle Co.,Ltd was founded in 1995 by U Ye Myint Maung and five siblings together.

U Ye Myint Maung is the managing director and the others fours of his siblings are the shareholders(Board of Directors) of Myanmar Belle Co.,Ltd. U Ye Myint Maung is the central executive committee at the republic of the union of Myanmar Federation Of Chambers Of Commerce and Industry (UMFCCI). He is also general secretary in Myanmar Food Processors and Exporters Association (MFPEA). He started founding Myanmar Belle in 1995 with primary focus on export and import where it eventually grew larger and now reorganized as Myanmar Belle Group of Companies. With the strong will of wanting to establish a name of “Made in Myanmar”, he is constantly working on ways of improving the quality and services that Myanmar Belle is providing in every aspect.
