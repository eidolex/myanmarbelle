Agriculture

Our concept of Myanmar Belle Co.,Ltd is “Farm to table.” It is to supply safety and healthy products to consumers. We practice a contract farming system to be traceable of raw materials to supply safety and healthy products to consumer not only local market but also export market in accordance with foods standard.

We perform the Good Agriculture Practices and better monitoring record, especially it is secure for the pesticide residues.

Technical Support & Providing Raw Necessity

Via Micro-finance, farmers receive financial aids and are provided basic materials for farming such as seeds, composts, etc., as well as supporting 80% of expenses needed for farming. Employees are provided vocational training overseas, in addition to agri-student supported with scholarships for academic achievements since 2014.

Geographical location for Farm

Geographical environment, climate, soil and other factors determine the level of food ingredients.
